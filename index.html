Who we are

Diving club is an active and rising scuba diving club with and proud to be an authorised PADI 5 Star Dive Centre and PADI Approved Youth Training Centre. Our club offers full courses for divers and non-divers. More than 20 years of experience in organizing courses and participating in professional missions across globe, we are here to provide best experience in most amazing oceans of the world.
